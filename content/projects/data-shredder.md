# Data Shredder

*2025 — Interactive installation*

Data Shredder is an interactive installation that physically enacts the impossibility of digital erasure. Visitors feed printed documents — fragments of their own browsing history, location data, chat logs — into a modified paper shredder whose output feeds back into a screen, where the "shredded" content is algorithmically reconstructed in real time.

---

## Concept

The work questions our intuitive understanding of deletion. In physical space, shredding is terminal. In digital space, destruction is always partial — data persists in backups, caches, metadata, inference. The installation uses this gap as its primary material.

## Process

The reconstruction algorithm is deliberately imperfect, producing something adjacent to the original rather than identical to it — a digital echo rather than a revival.

## Exhibition

First shown as part of the Club Noord × Kunstkantine group show at Minerva Art Academy, Groningen, 2025.
