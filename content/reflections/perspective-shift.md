# Perspective Shift

Something shifted this week. Not dramatically — more like a slow tilt of the frame through which I've been seeing the work.

I've been sitting with the question of whether the data I collect is *mine* in any meaningful sense. These digital footprints — location history, message timestamps, Spotify plays at 3am — they're generated by me, but shaped entirely by systems I didn't design. At what point does the archive become a collaboration I never agreed to?

---

The installation is starting to respond differently to the input. There's a latency I didn't plan for, a small delay between action and reaction that feels more honest than instant feedback ever did. I'm keeping it.
